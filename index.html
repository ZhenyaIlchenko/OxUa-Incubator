OxUA Incubator is an online platform in Oxford that accelerates Ukrainian startups and facilitates cooperation between the UK and Ukraine in the digital and technological sectors, selecting and supporting promising Ukrainian startups with academic, financial and mentorship resources.

OxUA Incubator accelerates promising Ukrainian startups with academic, financial, and mentorship resources, promoting UK-Ukraine cooperation in tech, specifically in education and health sectors

20+ Partners and Mentors

Mission:

We aim to foster a dynamic and inclusive startup ecosystem in Ukraine by connecting entrepreneurs, investors, mentors, and educators across UK and US communities to support innovative and transformative concepts prioritizing sustainable growth. We prioritize biotech, health, and educational startups to drive positive change and foster economic development.

Vision:

Our vision is to establish Ukraine as a global leader in innovation and entrepreneurship by empowering startups that drive economic growth and societal impact, restoring Ukraine's economy and communities in the wake of war.

Values:

Bridge: We believe in building bridges between stakeholders to create a cohesive network that fosters collaboration and knowledge sharing.

Unite: We are committed to bringing together diverse perspectives, experiences, and backgrounds to create an inclusive community that supports each other's success.

Innovate: We prioritize innovative and transformative concepts that have the potential to disrupt industries and drive positive change.

By living out our values of Bridge, Unite, Innovate, we will provide startups with the resources, support, and network they need to thrive and establish Ukraine as a leader in innovation and entrepreneurship.

What we do:

Select the most promising UA-based start-ups and provide them

Funding Solutions

We act as intermediaries between the selected UA-based start-ups and VC funds and investors

Mentorship and Business Guidance

We provide risk-adjusted legal and business advice to selected start-ups

Networking opportunities

We connect start-ups with investors, mentors and advisors. Also, we arrange and coordinate networking events, start-up crash tests, mentorship and advisors

Objectives and Key Industries:

Supporting Ukraine’s educational and health start-ups and contributing to Ukraine's economy

Bridging Ukraine's IT industry and UK's investment opportunities

Facilitation of UA-UK economic relationships
